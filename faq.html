How do purchase order on the site?

To order your purchase:-
1. Click “Add to cart” button on the products you want to purchase.
2. Click “Checkout” to proceed your payment for the products you want to purchase.
3. You will reach a log in page where you need to log in your account by using your existing Username and Password. You need to register an account before you proceed your payment if you don’t have any account in our website.
4. Then you will need to confirm your shipping methods and click “next” button.
5. Next, you will need to choose your payment methods on the 3 radio buttons which is ‘credit card’ , ‘online banking’ and ‘cash on delivery’.
6. After the steps before this have been done. You need to reconfirm your billing details for the products you want to purchase and agree on the terms and condition.
7. Click “Confirm Order” to complete the order.

If you forgot your Password for your account. What should you do to retrieve it?

1.Click on "Lost Password?" and fill in your email that used to register the account in the box. Then click "Submit" button.
2.A verification code will be sent to you. Once you have received the code, you will be able to change a new password for your account.

How long does the delivery usually takes?

Delivery for ready stock order usually will be takes about 3 to 5 days. Order which included pre-order will be shipped around estimate time arrival(ETA).

Can I amend my order?

Yes. Only if the order is haven't made payment and still on pending status. For order have been proceed and payment are not allowed to amend the order.

Can I combine my new order with other order?

No. Combine order is strictly not allowed because in order at the soonest possible and to prevent any mishandling of order.

Can I cancel the existing order that have been made?

Yes. But only the order is still on pending status and haven't made payment. Please send an email to our customer service at uniqueSD@gmail.com with the message title "Request to cancel order" in order to inform us about the cancellation order. Orders that are have made payment and in shipping or shipped will not be cancel.
